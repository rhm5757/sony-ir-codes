Sony CD/Cassette/Tuner (12, 13, 14, 16, 17, 18, 144, 26.57, 26.153)

 

This is one of those bookshelf audio systems or mini systems or whatever you call them.  They really don’t have a name.  Anyway, these things use a lot of codes from other devices plus the 26.57 stuff.  Incomplete.

 

Note that I added a bunch of codes for older systems that have multiple sets of transport keys (play, stop, etc.)  Most recent systems use only the codes in device 26.57, though the others might work in some limited way.
