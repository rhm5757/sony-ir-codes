Sony “Dream System” (12, 13, 80, 208, 16.16, 26.73)

 

The Sony “Dream System” uses a lot of device codes, not surprisingly, due to it being lots of devices in one.  Devices 80, 208 and 16.16, the three “new” ones, now make up the DVD/Receiver combo device.  Most of these commands are now on other pages. 

 

Note that devices 12 and 13 are only used by older models.  Newer ones had those codes remapped to one of the other devices.
