Sony Playstation 2 (26.218)

 

These are the codes from the PS2 remote learned to a CCF.  Keep in mind that you need the IR receiver to use these codes.  Many commands on the remote are under the DVD device (26.73), and a list of them is at the bottom.

 

The original Sony PS2 remote sends the arrow key commands twice, once under the 26.73 device (commands 121-124) and once under the 26.218 device (commands 84-87).

 

The power and eject commands only work on the newer models.
