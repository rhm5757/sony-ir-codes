Sony Receiver (16, 13, 12, 18, 24, 144, 16.0, 16.32, 16.96, 121, 25.11, 25.35, 25.43; 48, 45, 176, 16.8, 16.40, 16.104)

 

Sony receivers use a lot of different device codes.  These codes seem to have been put together all different ways, and there are several sets of numbers and inputs.  The device codes roughly correspond to a type of function, for example, device code 13 is for tuner functions.  The phono and some cassette codes are also in here.  These codes come from many different sources, including a lot of older ones from OFA advanced codes sheets.  Update: Thanks to eBay, I have gotten hold of a RM-PP505 and learned the AV2 mode codes.  They are the normal device code plus 32, and are listed after the semicolon.  I also got the 3rd room codes off of this.

 

Green = works on newer receivers
