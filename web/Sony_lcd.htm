Sony LCD Monitor (1.0, 4.5)

 

This is interesting because it uses the TV codes, however they are 20 bits long instead of the 12 or 15 the normal TV codes are.  They just pad the end with zeros.  It ends up being 1.0 and 4.5 instead of 1 and 164, respectively.
