Sony TV early DTV Tuner codes (26.26)

 

OK, so I think I know what this is now.  It is a separate set of codes for the DTV tuner of some early model HDTVs.  The only models I know of that use these are the KWP65HD1 and the KW34HD1.  All this stuff is from RemoteCentral.
